# Strategic Decision SD-001

## Title

Primary Market Positioning

## Status

Approved

## Date

2026-07-06

## Decision

The job search strategy will prioritize positions in the following domains:

- Digital Manufacturing
- Manufacturing Systems
- Manufacturing Software
- MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems)
- Smart Manufacturing
- Industrial Data Engineering

General-purpose Data Engineering and Business Intelligence roles will not be the primary search target. They may be considered only when they represent a strong fit with manufacturing, industrial automation, or smart factory environments.

## Rationale

The candidate's strongest competitive advantage is not software development alone, nor manufacturing experience alone.

It is the combination of:

- 22 years of manufacturing experience at Toyota.
- Strong software engineering capabilities.
- Data Engineering and Analytics skills.
- Industrial process knowledge.
- Two portfolio projects specifically focused on Manufacturing Analytics and Smart Manufacturing.

A comprehensive LinkedIn Jobs market study confirmed that the highest concentration of opportunities aligned with this profile belongs to the Digital Manufacturing ecosystem rather than generic Data Engineering.

Targeting this niche:

- reduces competition from generalist software candidates;
- increases differentiation during resume screening;
- maximizes portfolio relevance;
- increases expected salary;
- improves interview probability.

## Official LinkedIn Jobs Search Categories

The official search categories for market research are:

- Manufacturing Systems Engineer
- Manufacturing Applications Engineer
- Manufacturing Software Engineer
- Digital Manufacturing Engineer
- Manufacturing Intelligence
- MES Engineer
- Smart Manufacturing Engineer
- Industrial Data Engineer

These categories constitute the standard methodology for future LinkedIn Jobs market analyses.

## Exceptions

Generic searches (e.g. Data Engineer, Data Analyst, BI Developer, Analytics Engineer) should only be performed when:

- validating market trends;
- comparing salary potential;
- exploring opportunities inside previously selected target companies.

They must not become the primary search strategy.

## Core Value Proposition

Designing industrial decision systems that transform manufacturing data into actionable operational intelligence.

## Expected Outcome

Maximize the probability of obtaining international positions in the USD 40k–70k range while leveraging the candidate's unique industrial background and minimizing direct competition with generalist software profiles.
