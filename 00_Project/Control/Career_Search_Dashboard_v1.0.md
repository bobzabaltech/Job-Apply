# Career Search Dashboard v1.0

## Purpose

Provide an executive view of the international career transition project.

The objective is to measure progress toward the final goal:

Obtaining a high-value international opportunity aligned with Julian Cabrera Marceglia's Manufacturing Systems positioning.

---

# Main Objective

Maximize:

* interview probability;
* market credibility;
* compensation potential;
* speed to offer.

---

# Funnel Overview

## Market Discovery

Measure:

* Companies identified.
* Relevant roles found.
* New target markets discovered.

---

## Applications

Measure:

* Applications submitted.
* CV variant used.
* Role family.
* Geographic market.

---

## Conversations

Measure:

* Recruiter contacts.
* Networking conversations.
* Hiring manager interactions.

---

## Interviews

Measure:

* HR interviews.
* Technical interviews.
* Final interviews.

---

## Offers

Measure:

* Total compensation.
* Location.
* Relocation requirements.
* Strategic value.

---

# Core Metrics

## Weekly Activity

Track:

* New opportunities analyzed.
* Applications submitted.
* Contacts initiated.

---

## Conversion Metrics

Calculate:

Application → Response

Response → Interview

Interview → Offer

---

# Strategic Signals

## Positive Signals

Examples:

* Recruiters identify Manufacturing Systems fit.
* Interest in industrial data experience.
* Interviews generated from target roles.

Action:

Increase investment.

---

## Negative Signals

Examples:

* Market consistently categorizes profile as traditional Quality.
* Low response from target roles.
* Salary expectations mismatch.

Action:

Review positioning.

---

# Decision Framework

Every 4 weeks evaluate:

## Continue

What is working?

## Change

What creates weak results?

## Stop

What consumes time without signal?

---

# Success Definition

The project succeeds when:

* target companies recognize the positioning;
* interviews are generated consistently;
* compensation aligns with objectives;
* a strategic offer is obtained.
