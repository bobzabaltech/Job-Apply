# Market Validation Sprint v1.0

## Objective

Validate the international market response to Julian Cabrera Marceglia's professional positioning.

The purpose is to determine which market narrative generates the strongest demand.

---

# Validation Period

Initial sprint:

3-4 weeks.

---

# Main Hypotheses

## H1 — Manufacturing Systems Positioning

Message:

Manufacturing professional capable of designing and improving industrial systems.

Target roles:

* Manufacturing Systems Engineer.
* MES Engineer.
* Factory Systems Engineer.

---

## H2 — Digital Manufacturing Positioning

Message:

Manufacturing engineer specialized in digital transformation.

Target roles:

* Digital Manufacturing Engineer.
* Industry 4.0 Engineer.
* Smart Factory Engineer.

---

## H3 — Industrial Data Positioning

Message:

Manufacturing professional who builds systems turning industrial data into engineering decisions.

Target roles:

* Industrial Data Engineer.
* Manufacturing Intelligence Engineer.

---

# Initial Sample

Total opportunities:

15.

Distribution:

* 5 Industrial Software.
* 5 Automotive.
* 5 Digital Manufacturing.

---

# Success Metrics

Track:

* Applications sent.
* Responses received.
* Recruiter conversations.
* Interviews.
* Market feedback.
* Role terminology used.

---

# Decision Rules

After first sprint:

If one positioning generates significantly higher response:

Increase investment in that segment.

If all segments perform poorly:

Review:

* CV.
* LinkedIn.
* Target companies.
* Search strategy.

---

# Output

The sprint must produce:

* validated positioning;
* improved targeting;
* market terminology;
* next application strategy.
