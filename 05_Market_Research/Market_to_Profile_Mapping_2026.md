# Market to Profile Mapping 2026

## Purpose

This document translates market requirements identified in:

* LinkedIn Jobs Market Analysis 2026;
* Employer Intelligence Database;
* Employer Scoring Model;
* Target 50;

into a candidate positioning framework.

The objective is to ensure that:

* CV versions;
* LinkedIn profile;
* application messages;
* interview answers;

are aligned with the market demand identified through evidence.

---

# Strategic Positioning

## Core Professional Identity

The candidate should be positioned as:

> Digital Manufacturing / Manufacturing Systems professional combining deep industrial experience with software and data capabilities to build industrial decision systems.

---

# Positioning Principles

## Do not compete as:

### Generic Software Engineer

Reason:

* high competition;
* weak differentiation;
* underuses industrial experience.

---

### Generic Data Engineer

Reason:

* does not communicate manufacturing expertise;
* loses connection with operational problems.

---

## Compete as:

### Industrial Decision Systems Professional

Combining:

* manufacturing knowledge;
* quality systems;
* software engineering;
* industrial data;
* analytics;
* operational improvement.
