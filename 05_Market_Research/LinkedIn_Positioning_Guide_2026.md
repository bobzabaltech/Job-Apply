# LinkedIn Positioning Guide 2026

## Purpose

This document defines the strategy to optimize the candidate's LinkedIn profile according to the target market identified in:

* LinkedIn Jobs Market Analysis 2026;
* Employer Intelligence Database;
* Employer Scoring Model;
* Candidate Positioning Framework 2026.

The objective is to transform the current LinkedIn profile from a quality/metrology-oriented profile into a Digital Manufacturing / Manufacturing Systems profile while preserving the strongest differentiator:

> Deep manufacturing experience combined with software and industrial data capabilities.

---

The current profile communicates:

* SPC;
