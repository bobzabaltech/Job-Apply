# Target 50

## Purpose

This document defines the prioritized employer target list for the Apply Jobs project.

The objective is not to maximize the number of applications.

The objective is to concentrate effort on companies where the candidate has the highest probability of converting:

* manufacturing experience;
* software capabilities;
* industrial data knowledge;
* digital manufacturing expertise;

into international career opportunities.

---

# Selection Methodology

Companies are selected using:

```text id="m8j5di"
Employer Strategic Fit Score

+

Market Evidence

+

Candidate Differentiation

+

Case Study Alignment
```

The ranking prioritizes:

1. Manufacturing complexity.
2. Digital transformation maturity.
3. Industrial systems needs.
4. Alignment with Industrial Decision Systems positioning.
5. Probability that the candidate's experience creates differentiation.

# Important Strategy Rule

The candidate should not apply using generic software positioning.

Applications should emphasize:

```text id="d9u2p0"
Manufacturing Experience

+

Digital Systems

+

Industrial Data

+

Operational Improvement
```

The strongest differentiator is not software alone.

The differentiator is the ability to understand industrial problems and build digital solutions.
