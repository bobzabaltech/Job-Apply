# Target Company Matrix v1.0

## Purpose

Identify and prioritize companies where Julian Cabrera Marceglia's combination of:

* Automotive manufacturing experience.
* Industrial data management.
* Digital manufacturing capabilities.
* Software engineering knowledge.

creates a competitive market advantage.

The objective is not maximizing application quantity.

The objective is maximizing probability of obtaining high-value international opportunities.

---

# Scoring Model

Each company is evaluated from 1 to 5.

## Criteria

| Factor                          | Weight |
| ------------------------------- | -----: |
| Manufacturing Systems Fit       |    25% |
| Digital Manufacturing Relevance |    20% |
| Industrial Data Alignment       |    20% |
| Compensation Potential          |    20% |
| International Accessibility     |    15% |

Formula:

```
Company Score =
(Fit × 0.25)
+
(Digital × 0.20)
+
(Data × 0.20)
+
(Compensation × 0.20)
+
(Accessibility × 0.15)
```

# Priority Tier Definition

## Tier A

Immediate targeting.

Characteristics:

* Strong manufacturing ecosystem.
* Clear digital transformation initiatives.
* High probability of valuing industrial experience.

## Tier B

Selective targeting.

Characteristics:

* Good alignment.
* More competition.
* Requires stronger application customization.

## Tier C

Opportunistic.

Characteristics:

* Possible fit but lower strategic advantage.
