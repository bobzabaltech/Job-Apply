About the job

About The Role

At ARX Robotics, the quality and traceability of manufacturing data is mission-critical. As Manufacturing Digitalization Specialist, you will take ownership of capturing, structuring, and digitalizing all relevant data generated across our production environment. From robotic platform configurations and assembly stages to component tracking and build status visibility.

You act as the critical link between the shop floor and our SAP S/4HANA system, translating physical production realities into clean, reliable, and actionable data. This role sits at the intersection of production operations and IT, and requires someone equally comfortable in both worlds: familiar enough with manufacturing to understand what happens on the floor, and technically capable of bringing that information into structured, scalable digital processes.

As ARX Robotics scales production of its autonomous ground systems, you will own the data foundation that makes it possible.

What You'll Build

    Capture and digitalize production data across all manufacturing stages, including platform build status, component usage (e.g. parts, screws, subassemblies), and robot system configurations
    Maintain and continuously improve production-related data structures in SAP S/4HANA, including production orders, routings, work centers, and material movements
    Ensure full traceability of each unit across all production stages, from initial assembly through to final system integration and handover
    Systematically collect and consolidate shop floor data to feed continuous improvement processes, identifying patterns, deviations, and optimization potential across production workflows
    Ensure that relevant digital information is available directly at the workstation, enabling operators to work efficiently and with full context
    Collaborate closely with Production, Engineering, and Quality to identify data gaps, define data capture standards, and ensure process-aligned documentation
    Support the setup and optimization of digital workflows that replace manual or paper-based data collection on the shop floor
    Monitor data quality and completeness across connected systems, proactively flagging inconsistencies, and serve as a go-to contact for SAP-related questions
    Contribute to the development of production dashboards and KPIs (e.g. via Power BI or similar tools) to make manufacturing progress and bottlenecks visible in real time

What You Bring

    Fluency in English is required, as well as basic conversational German (B1 or above preferred)
    Completed degree or apprenticeship in industrial engineering, business informatics, mechatronics, production technology, or a comparable field
    Several years of professional experience in a production or manufacturing environment, with hands-on exposure to operational data capture and process documentation
    Working knowledge of SAP sufficient to maintain data, trigger transactions, and support process integration; prior SAP certification is not required
    Strong IT affinity and the ability to quickly learn new systems, tools, and digital workflows
    Structured and detail-oriented working style with a genuine interest in making complex production processes transparent and traceable
    Proactive personality who is comfortable spending time on the shop floor and translating observations into well-structured data
    Good communication skills with the ability to collaborate across technical and non-technical teams

Please note: You do not need to meet every single requirement to apply. We welcome motivated candidates who are eager to grow into the role and develop their expertise further.

What We Offer

Join a mission-driven European defense tech company at a pivotal stage of growth, where you can help shape meaningful technology, take ownership early, and grow with the business

About the job

Job Description:

An opportunity for an Industrial System Engineer has arisen within Airbus Operations, Getafe (Madrid).

The selected candidate will join the ME - Transversal Team, a key part of the Manufacturing Engineering organization at the Airbus Getafe Plant.

Our vision is clear: "to secure the industrial system today and to create the sustainable industrial solution of the future."

The Getafe Plant is a center of excellence specializing in the structural assembly and equipping of Horizontal Tail Planes (HTPs) for the A320, A330, and A350 families, as well as the S19 section for the A350, utilizing advanced automated and robotic methods and dry carbon fiber manufacturing.

In this role, your core mission will be to predict and improve our Industrial Performance by developing Lean Industrial System Designs, optimizing flows, and building advanced simulation models to translate conceptual ideas into highly competitive, real-world shopfloor scenarios.

Responsibilities

The main tasks of the jobholder will be:

     Lead and conduct Industrial System Design and Modelling projects from initial concept to deployment, optimizing flows and ensuring the best industrial footprint for plant activities. 
     Develop and manage Industrial Line Flow Simulations and Digital Twins using advanced 3D software (e.g., 3DExperience) to anticipate, replicate, and optimize assembly line behavior. 
     Analyze and optimize the industrial performance of current and future manufacturing lines through time-value calculations, standard line balancing, and Value Stream Analysis deployment. 
     Drive the Machine Connectivity strategy at the plant level, collaborating closely with Maintenance, Operations, and central teams to define strategies, secure budgets, and improve machine availability. 
     Support and deploy Data Analytics projects and innovation technologies, managing data to enable predictive decision-making, pattern detection, and early breakdown warnings. 
     Collaborate with Industrial Architects and multidisciplinary teams to translate conceptual workflows into real shopfloor realities, factoring in surfaces, logistics, lead times, and station-level ergonomics. 

Skills

Is your profile aligned to the following list of skills? Apply! We are looking forward to knowing you:

     Education: University Degree in Engineering (Industrial, Aeronautical, or a related discipline). 
     Experience: Proven experience in Manufacturing Engineering, preferably within complex industrial, automated, or aerospace environments. 
     Soft skills: High passion, energy, and motivation for continuous improvement. Strong teamwork spirit, creativity, integrity, reliability, and the leadership potential to manage transversal projects. 
     Languages: Negotiation level in English is a must. Capitalization of another European language is valuable. 
     Technical knowledge: Strong proficiency in 3D environments (such as 3DExperience) for layouts, line simulation, and logistics analysis. Knowledge of flow simulation methodologies and data management. 
     Valuable / Nice to have: Knowledge of AnyLogic software is a strong plus. Experience in digital twin deployment, machine connectivity (IoT), or advanced data analytics.

About the job

Gestalte die digitale Zukunft der Life-Sciences-Produktion! Als Manufacturing Systems Specialist mit PAS-X Fokus unterstützt du die Einführung und Optimierung von Manufacturing Execution Systems und trägst aktiv zur Digitalisierung von Fertigungsprozessen bei.

Deine Mission

Du konzipierst und konfigurierst MES-Lösungen (PAS-X) und begleitest Anwender durch praxisnahe Trainings – für effiziente, GMP-konforme Produktionsprozesse.

Aufgaben


    Gestalten & Umsetzen

    Du spezifizierst, entwickelst und implementierst MES-Lösungen, erstellst elektronische Batch Records (EBRs) und Master Batch Records (MBRs) und sorgst für die Integration mit ERP-Systemen und Shopfloor-Technologien.

    Validieren, Testen & Dokumentieren

    Abhängig von Deiner Spezialisierung ist auch eine Fokussierung auf Validierung und Compliance-Aspekte darstellbar im Sinne der Sicherstellung von GMP- sowie FDA-Richtlinien.

    Zusammenarbeiten & Vorantreiben

    Du arbeitest eng mit Prozess-Experten und Stakeholdern zusammen, begleitest Change-Prozesse und teilst dein Wissen als Mentor im Team.


Qualifikation


    Du hast ein abgeschlossenes Studium in Naturwissenschaften, Ingenieurwesen oder einem vergleichbaren technischen Bereich

    Du bringst mindestens 3 Jahre praktische Erfahrung mit PAS-X (MBR-Design und/oder Training) mit

    Du kennst dich mit MES-Plattformen wie PAS-X, Syncade, PharmaSuite oder Camstar aus

    Du bist vertraut mit GMP-/FDA-Richtlinien und Validierungsprozessen

    Du beherrschst die deutschen Sprache fließend und kannst komplexe Inhalte verständlich vermitteln

    Du bringst für Workshops und Trainings eine temporäre Reisebereitschaft mit


Benefits


    Ein flexibles Arbeitsmodell, bei dem du die Möglichkeit hast, mobil zu arbeiten und deine Arbeitszeit aktiv selbst zu gestalten

    Zugriff auf umfassende Weiterbildungsmöglichkeiten zu den neusten Technologie- Trends sowie Soft Skills, damit du immer up-to-date bleibst

    Diverse sowie gleichberechtigte Teammitglieder liegen uns sehr am Herzen und um den Team-Zusammenhalt zu stärken, fördern wir regelmäßig Events und Veranstaltungen für dich und dein Team

    Ein attraktives und marktkonformes Vergütungspaket, welches du durch individuelle Zielerreichung selbst gestalten kannst

    Weitere Benefits, die unserer Meinung nach zum Standard gehören, aber nicht vergessen werden sollten: Mitarbeitenden-Rabatte, die Möglichkeit ein Job-Rad oder einen PkW zu Firmenkonditionen zu leasen, das Partizipieren an unserem Aktienprogramm, Versicherungs- und Altersvorsorge-Möglichkeiten und noch vieles mehr…


Du und Accenture

In dieser Rolle bist du Teil von Accenture Industry X. Wir verbinden #Engineering und #Manufacturing mit Daten und Technologien. So befähigen wir unsere Kunden, ihre Produkt- und Wertschöpfungsketten mit digitalen Zwillingen, intelligenten Anlagen und vernetzten Fertigungsprozessen neu auszurichten – für die Industrie der Zukunft.

Die Accenture-Kultur: Hier machst DU den Unterschied

Deine Fähigkeiten, dein Naturell und deine Ambitionen machen dich einzigartig. Und wenn die unterschiedlichsten Talente, Stärken und Sichtweisen aufeinandertreffen, entsteht etwas Großartiges: Ideen, die in der Welt etwas bewegen. Deshalb setzen wir bei Accenture auf Diversität und freie Entfaltung. Schaffe dir ein Arbeitsumfeld, in dem du aufgehst. Mit Aufgaben, die dir am Herzen liegen. Mit einem Workload, der zu dir passt. Und mit einer Arbeitsweise, die sich an deinen Zielen orientiert. Wie du dich vernetzen, weiterbilden und persönlich wachsen willst, liegt bei dir. Es ist deine Karriere. Wir helfen dir, sie so zu gestalten, wie du es dir vorstellst.

Dein Kontakt

Du hast Fragen? Dann wende dich gerne an Clara - wir freuen uns auf deine Bewerbung!

About the job

The Impact You'll Make

Danfoss is proud to be at the forefront of driving a global transformation in digital and electric solutions that increase productivity and energy efficiency. We are truly passionate about investment, and innovation, as we continue to support our customers' journey towards a greener and lower-carbon future.

We now havee an opportunity to recruit a Manufacturing System and Solution Consultant to join the EMEA region within Danfoss Power Solutions (DPS) Global Operations (GOPS). As an integral part of the manufacturing delivery team, you will be working alongside a highly skilled team of global solution architects and designers, innovating the technology and MFG systems used to build sustainable smart solutions that power industries and cities.

As a member of GOPS Manufacturing Systems and Services, you work with business stakeholders to troubleshoot incidents, resolve problems, gather requirements and implement technical system solutions. Business stakeholders are typically Manufacturing Engineers, Process Technicians, Equipment Operators or members of leadership. To improve the safety, quality, and productivity of our manufacturing environment, technical solutions range from standard PC’s with commercially available software, to purpose-built MES systems with custom hardware and software solutions. This role regularly requires researching new systems, applying existing standards to them and creating documentation. To succeed in this position, it is essential to understand the manufacturing shop floor landscape and execution layers (ISA95).

With this position, the right candidate will be offered the chance to lead integration projects across sites and support future growth and strategy – a great career opportunity.

This role will require a base at one of our main manufacturing plants in EMEA such as Nordborg (Denmark), Neumunster (Germany) or Povaszka (Slovakia), with up to 40 days travel per year anticipated.

What You’ll Be Doing

    Provide in-person and remote support for the enhancement, development, implementation, migration, and upgrade of manufacturing systems and services.
    Create documentation for installation, support, and training purposes.
    Work across multiple IT groups to provide support and deliver solutions within manufacturing.
    Develop and share best practices related to industry knowledge, technology relevance, and target solutions.
    Engages with local leadership regularly to foster positive, cross-functional relationships with IT and non-IT colleagues.
    Support the integration of MES, HMI, and ERP solutions.
    On-call support as required
    Willing to initiate and take on finding processes/solutions to make team improvements

What We're Looking For

    Degree qualification within a Manufacturing or Information Technology discipline, alongside a demonstrable track record of success in supporting manufacturing/shop floor systems
    Familiarity of ISA-95 and ITIL models and concepts
    Experience with Azure Dev Ops or similar development tools
    C#, VB, .Net web services/framework preferred, or experience with PLCs and Scada systems
    Basic SSRS, SQL Database knowledge, Oracle Database familiarity is a plus but not required
    MSSQL, TSQL, Stored Procedures, Indexing proficient with DB’s
    Experience working with networking, server, and local site service teams
    Strong working knowledge of current operating systems, IT hardware, networking concepts
    Effective communication skills and an ability to offer technical education to those who are peers as well as those in leadership

What You'll Get From Us

    We promote from within and support your learning with mentoring, training, and access to global opportunities.
    You’ll have flexibility, autonomy, and support to do your best work while maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Your well-being matters to us.
    We strive to create an inclusive work environment where people of all backgrounds are respected, and valued for who they are. 
    You’ll receive benefits like annual bonus, paid vacation, pension plans, personal insurance, and more. These vary by country and contract, but they’re worth asking about—we think they’re pretty great.

Danfoss engineers solutions that increase machine productivity, reduce emissions, lower energy consumption, and enable electrification.

Our solutions are used in such areas as refrigeration, air conditioning, heating, power conversion, motor control, industrial machinery, automotive, marine, and off- and on-highway equipment. We also provide solutions for renewable energy, such as solar and wind power, as well as district-energy infrastructure for cities.

Our innovative engineering dates back to 1933. Danfoss is family-owned, employing more than 39,000 people, serving customers in more than 100 countries through a global footprint of 100 factories.

About the job

Senior Data Consultant

We are seeking a Senior Specialist – Industry 4.0 & Manufacturing Data to act as a technical authority and strategic advisor across digital manufacturing initiatives. This role blends deep technical expertise, regulated-industry rigor, and client-facing leadership, supporting both delivery excellence and the growth of Deep Digital’s Industry 4.0 services.

Location: Remote - Ireland

Main Responsibilities:

    Act as a trusted senior advisor to clients on Industry 4.0, manufacturing data, and digital transformation
    Lead development of digital transformation strategies and roadmaps, including:
        Manufacturing Data Platforms & Historians
        Unified Namespace (UNS) and event-driven architectures
        OT/IT integration and cloud/hybrid data strategies



    Guide technology selection and architecture across:
        Data integration, streaming, and contextualisation platforms
        MES, LIMS, QMS, CMMS, and Enterprise integrations
        Control systems and OT platforms 
        Advanced analytics, AI/ML, and digital twin use cases
    Embed data, validation, and governance strategies into project designs in collaboration with internal engineering teams and client stakeholders
    Lead small, multidisciplinary teams delivering projects from concept through design, validation, and handover.

Job benefits:

    Hybrid working
    22 days Annual Leave
    5% Pension
    Paid Sick days
    Income Protection
    Life Insurance
    Professional development


Qualiﬁcations:

    Bachelor’s or master's degree in data science, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related ﬁeld and 6+ years of relevant experience. Where a degree is not held—significant professional experience that demonstrates the ability to excel in this role.

    Deep experience/understanding across Industry 4.0 domains, including:
        Control systems, automation, and OT environments. 
        Manufacturing data historians and platforms
        IT/OT integration, networking, and cybersecurity
        Cloud and hybrid infrastructure
        Vertical and horizontal system integration (MES–ERP–Enterprise)



    Strong understanding of:
        Data warehousing concepts, ETL/ELT processes, and data governance models
        ISA-88 and ISA-95 standards for batch and manufacturing operations
        FAIR data principles and data integrity requirements (ALCOA+)
    Solid experience working within GAMP 5 frameworks and Computer Systems Validation (CSV) in regulated manufacturing environments

Preferred Skills:

    Excellent verbal, written, and presentation skills, with the ability to communicate complex concepts to both technical and executive audiences
    Demonstrated ability to manage multiple concurrent projects, prioritise effectively, and meet deadlines
    Strong problem-solving skills with high attention to detail
    Understanding of ontologies and semantic models and their role in data standardisation and interoperability
    Experience with data visualisation or analytics tools (e.g. Power BI, Tableau, Grafana)
    Hands-on experience with SQL, Python, R, or similar data-focused programming languages
    Exposure to PAT, real-time release, predictive analytics, or closed-loop control use cases

About the job

About Us

Harmattan AI is a next-generation defense prime building autonomous and scalable defense systems. Following the close of a $200M Series B, valuing the company at $1.4 billion, we are expanding our teams and capabilities to deliver mission-critical systems to allied forces.

Our work is guided by clear values: building technologies with real-world impact, pursuing excellence in everything we do, setting ambitious goals, and taking on the hardest technical challenges. We operate in a demanding environment where rigor, ownership, and execution are expected.

About The Role

The Manufacturing Automation & Digitalization Engineer is a key member of the Automation & Industrialization (A&I) team and a central architect of Harmattan AI's digital manufacturing backbone at its greenfield Wissous facility.

This role coordinates the digitalization of production lines through the definition and integration of MES and ERP architectures, acting as the primary bridge between engineering, production, quality, and supply chain. Working in close partnership with the Odoo Software Engineer, you co-own the digital and operational architecture of the factory from day one.

You capture cross-functional operational needs, define system architecture, and translate requirements into actionable technical specifications — enabling scalable production, tighter quality loops, and full traceability of our autonomous defense systems.

Missions

Cross-Functional Requirements Coordination

    Lead structured operational analysis and requirements workshops with production, engineering, quality, and supply chain — mapping current and target operating modes at line and equipment level, identifying process gaps, and establishing a formal, baselined set of operational needs
    Build and maintain a traceable requirements repository — from stakeholder need to implemented feature — using standard requirements management practices
    Ensure continuous alignment between evolving operational requirements and the deployed system architecture

OT/IT System Architecture & Integration

    Co-define with the Odoo Software Engineer the end-to-end digital architecture of production lines: data flows, system interfaces, API contracts, and OT/IT integration layers
    Define the application landscape: Odoo MRP/MES scope, dedicated MES modules, automated equipment interfaces, IoT/edge connectivity, and data aggregation layer
    Specify connectivity architecture for automated machines and tools: robotic cells, automated test benches, measurement systems, and smart tooling
    Maintain a living architecture reference documenting decisions, interfaces, and data flows

Machine & Tool Connectivity

    Define data acquisition requirements for each automated asset: cycle times, process parameters, alarm states, tool wear indicators, yield and scrap data
    Read and interpret PLC programs, machine data maps, and equipment communication specifications to define precise integration requirements
    Specify edge computing and data normalization layer to aggregate heterogeneous machine data into a unified, ERP-consumable format
    Drive integration of automated test equipment (ATE) and measurement systems into the quality data pipeline — enabling automated pass/fail decisions and SPC data feeds
    Define smart tooling connectivity requirements: torque wrenches, automated dispensing systems, vision inspection systems, and coordinate measurement machines (CMM)
    Oversee machine onboarding process: protocol qualification, data mapping, signal validation, and end-to-end integration testing

Shop-Floor Digitalization

    Define and oversee implementation of digital work instructions and connected operator interfaces, synchronized with machine states and automated process steps
    Specify and deploy end-to-end component and assembly traceability: from goods receipt through automated WIP tracking to shipment, fully integrated with Odoo manufacturing and inventory modules
    Implement digital quality control points linked to automated measurement outputs, in-process inspection records, FAI workflows, and NCR management
    Define OEE data collection architecture: automated machine availability, performance, and quality data feeds into real-time production dashboards

Requirements

Essential Qualifications

    Engineering degree in Industrial Engineering, Manufacturing, Mechatronics, Computer Science, or equivalent
    3 to 6 years of hands-on experience in manufacturing digitalization, MES/ERP implementation, or industrial IT in a production environment
    Proven track record of eliciting and formalizing cross-functional requirements on industrial production lines
    Solid ability to define system architectures and integration flows (OT/IT, ERP, MES, IoT)
    Strong understanding of core manufacturing processes: routings, BoM structures, work orders, quality control
    Professional English required; French is a strong plus for daily on-site collaboration

About the job

The Impact You'll Make

Danfoss is proud to be at the forefront of driving a global transformation in digital and electric solutions that increase productivity and energy efficiency. We are truly passionate about investment, and innovation, as we continue to support our customers' journey towards a greener and lower-carbon future.

We now havee an opportunity to recruit a Manufacturing System and Solution Consultant to join the EMEA region within Danfoss Power Solutions (DPS) Global Operations (GOPS). As an integral part of the manufacturing delivery team, you will be working alongside a highly skilled team of global solution architects and designers, innovating the technology and MFG systems used to build sustainable smart solutions that power industries and cities.

As a member of GOPS Manufacturing Systems and Services, you work with business stakeholders to troubleshoot incidents, resolve problems, gather requirements and implement technical system solutions. Business stakeholders are typically Manufacturing Engineers, Process Technicians, Equipment Operators or members of leadership. To improve the safety, quality, and productivity of our manufacturing environment, technical solutions range from standard PC’s with commercially available software, to purpose-built MES systems with custom hardware and software solutions. This role regularly requires researching new systems, applying existing standards to them and creating documentation. To succeed in this position, it is essential to understand the manufacturing shop floor landscape and execution layers (ISA95).

With this position, the right candidate will be offered the chance to lead integration projects across sites and support future growth and strategy – a great career opportunity.

This role will require a base at one of our main manufacturing plants in EMEA such as Nordborg (Denmark), Neumunster (Germany) or Povaszka (Slovakia), with up to 40 days travel per year anticipated.

What You’ll Be Doing

    Provide in-person and remote support for the enhancement, development, implementation, migration, and upgrade of manufacturing systems and services.
    Create documentation for installation, support, and training purposes.
    Work across multiple IT groups to provide support and deliver solutions within manufacturing.
    Develop and share best practices related to industry knowledge, technology relevance, and target solutions.
    Engages with local leadership regularly to foster positive, cross-functional relationships with IT and non-IT colleagues.
    Support the integration of MES, HMI, and ERP solutions.
    On-call support as required
    Willing to initiate and take on finding processes/solutions to make team improvements

What We're Looking For

    Degree qualification within a Manufacturing or Information Technology discipline, alongside a demonstrable track record of success in supporting manufacturing/shop floor systems
    Familiarity of ISA-95 and ITIL models and concepts
    Experience with Azure Dev Ops or similar development tools
    C#, VB, .Net web services/framework preferred, or experience with PLCs and Scada systems
    Basic SSRS, SQL Database knowledge, Oracle Database familiarity is a plus but not required
    MSSQL, TSQL, Stored Procedures, Indexing proficient with DB’s
    Experience working with networking, server, and local site service teams
    Strong working knowledge of current operating systems, IT hardware, networking concepts
    Effective communication skills and an ability to offer technical education to those who are peers as well as those in leadership

What You'll Get From Us

    We promote from within and support your learning with mentoring, training, and access to global opportunities.
    You’ll have flexibility, autonomy, and support to do your best work while maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Your well-being matters to us.

    You’ll receive benefits like annual bonus, paid vacation, pension plans, personal insurance, and more. These vary by country and contract, but they’re worth asking about—we think they’re pretty great.

El Grupo Valeo es uno de los mayores proveedores de automoción del mundo; una empresa inspirada y centrada en productos innovadores, el desarrollo y la satisfacción del cliente. Trabajar con nosotros significa gestionar las tecnologías más avanzadas y sofisticadas.

Desde las últimas tecnologías de iluminación adaptativa hasta soluciones de iluminación interior intuitivas, pasando por una iluminación exterior elegante y centrada en el diseño, Valeo innova constantemente para mejorar la comodidad y la seguridad tanto de conductores como de pasajeros.

Como Ingeniero/a de VPS (Valeo Production System) en Valeo Martos, tendrás la oportunidad de trabajar con los fabricantes de automóviles más potentes en el desarrollo de electrónica, dentro de un entorno internacional y multicultural.

Estrategias VPS

    Asegurar que las reglas del VPS se tengan en cuenta en los nuevos proyectos en la fase adecuada.

    Gestionar el mantenimiento de las herramientas VPS mediante auditorías adecuadas (como auditorías Kanban): organización, ejecución y seguimiento.
    Preparar y organizar el comité semanal de VPS de la APU (incluyendo la propuesta de agenda) liderado por el Gerente de la APU, y redactar el acta.

    Apoyar a los pilotos de los talleres VPS de la APU en cuanto a metodología y consecución de resultados.
    Liderar permanentemente talleres e implementar propuestas de mejora.

🔩Yield strength = The first digit is multiplied by the second digit.
10*9=90 MPa 
So if you tighten it by 90MPa  more, the bolt will change shape and stretch.
