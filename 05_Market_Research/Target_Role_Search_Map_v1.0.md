# Target Role Search Map v1.0

## Purpose

Define the search vocabulary required to identify international opportunities aligned with Julian Cabrera Marceglia's target positioning.

The objective is to avoid dependence on a single job title and maximize discovery of relevant roles.

---

# Search Philosophy

Companies describe similar problems using different terminology.

The search strategy must focus on:

* Business problem.
* Manufacturing domain.
* Digital transformation.
* Industrial data.
* Engineering systems.

Not only job titles.

# Role Family 5 — Quality + Digital Hybrid

## Priority

B

## Purpose

Use only when the role contains significant digital/system components.

## Acceptable Titles

* Quality Systems Engineer
* Digital Quality Engineer
* Manufacturing Quality Systems Engineer
* Quality Data Engineer

## Avoid

* Quality Inspector
* Quality Technician
* Metrology Technician
* Traditional Quality Auditor

Reason:

These roles underutilize the target positioning.
