# CV Version Strategy 2026

## Purpose

This document defines the CV architecture strategy for maximizing international career opportunities.

The objective is not to create multiple unrelated resumes.

The objective is to maintain:

* one strong professional identity;
* multiple targeted presentations;
* maximum alignment with different market segments.

This strategy is based on:

* LinkedIn Jobs Market Analysis 2026;
* Target Roles;
* Employer Intelligence Database;
* Candidate Positioning Framework 2026;
* CV Positioning Audit 2026.

---

# Strategic Principle

The candidate should not compete as a generic:

* Software Engineer.
* Data Engineer.
* Quality Engineer.

The strongest market position is:

> Digital Manufacturing / Manufacturing Systems professional combining deep industrial experience with software and industrial data capabilities.

---

# CV Architecture

The recommended structure is:

```text
                    Master CV

                        |

        --------------------------------

        |              |               |

Digital Manufacturing   Industrial Data   MES / Manufacturing
Systems                 Intelligence       Applications
```

The three versions share the same foundation.

Only the emphasis changes.

---

# Master CV

## Purpose

The Master CV is the source document from which all targeted versions are created.

It should contain the complete professional value proposition.

---

# Master Identity

Recommended title:

> Digital Manufacturing & Industrial Data Specialist

Alternative:

> Manufacturing Systems Professional | Industrial Data | Digital Transformation

---

# Core Message

> Manufacturing professional with extensive industrial experience who combines software engineering and data capabilities to build digital systems that improve operational intelligence and decision-making.

---

# Core Evidence

The Master CV must always include:

## Industrial Experience

* Automotive manufacturing background.
* Toyota experience.
* Quality systems.
* Process improvement.
* Production environment knowledge.

---

## Digital Capabilities

* Software development.
* Data systems.
* Analytics.
* Industrial applications.

---

## Case Studies

Always include:

* Industrial Measurement Data Platform.
* Real-Time Quality Prioritization System.
