# Market Segments 2026

## Objective

Maximize market value and obtain the highest possible compensation in the shortest reasonable timeframe.

The selection of target segments is based on:

- Compensation potential
- Match with existing experience
- Probability of interview
- Market demand
- Time-to-offer

Segments that require significant reskilling, strong local-language dependency, or long positioning cycles are deprioritized.

---

# Segment 1 — Defense Tech

## Priority
A+

## Rationale

Defense technology companies are currently scaling rapidly across Europe due to increased military spending and industrial modernization.

Many organizations are building or expanding:

- Digital factories
- Smart manufacturing systems
- Industrial data platforms
- MES environments
- OT/IT integration capabilities
- Traceability systems
- Industrial automation

This creates strong demand for profiles combining:

- Manufacturing systems
- Industry 4.0
- Industrial data
- MES
- OT/IT integration

which closely matches the current positioning.

## Target Companies

- Harmattan AI
- ARX Robotics
- Quantum Systems
- Helsing
- Rheinmetall
- KNDS
- Saab
- Leonardo
- Anduril Industries
- Thales

## Expected Compensation

High to Very High

---

# Segment 2 — Industrial Software

## Priority
A+

## Rationale

Industrial software vendors sell the platforms that manufacturing companies use.

These organizations typically value:

- Industrial data expertise
- Manufacturing architecture knowledge
- OT/IT integration
- UNS concepts
- Historian platforms
- MES integrations

Competition is generally lower than in generic software engineering roles because domain expertise is required.

## Target Companies

- HighByte
- Inductive Automation
- Tulip
- Litmus
- AVEVA
- Rockwell Automation
- AspenTech

## Expected Compensation

High to Very High

---

# Segment 3 — MES / Manufacturing Software

## Priority
A

## Rationale

MES remains one of the strongest overlaps between existing experience, case studies, and market demand.

Organizations implementing or supporting MES platforms frequently require:

- Manufacturing process knowledge
- Data integration
- Traceability
- Shop-floor systems
- Industry 4.0 experience

The market is smaller than Industrial Software but presents strong fit.

## Target Companies

- Critical Manufacturing
- Siemens Digital Industries
- Camstar Ecosystem
- Opcenter Ecosystem
- Armacell
- AT&S
- Rolls-Royce Power Systems
- ABB
- Schneider Electric

## Expected Compensation

Medium-High to High

The fastest path toward high-compensation opportunities is currently:

Defense Tech
→ Industrial Software
→ MES / Manufacturing Software
