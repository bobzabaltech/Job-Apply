# Employer Intelligence Database

## Purpose

This document converts LinkedIn market evidence into a strategic employer intelligence layer.

The objective is not to create a list of companies.

The objective is to identify organizations where the candidate's combination of:

* manufacturing experience;
* software development;
* industrial data;
* digital manufacturing;
* operational decision systems;

has the highest probability of generating market value.

This document feeds:

* Employer Scoring Model;
* Target 50;
* application strategy;
* networking strategy.

# Employer Evaluation Framework

Companies are evaluated according to strategic fit.

The objective is not to maximize the number of applications.

# Employer Selection Principles

The following principles should guide future expansion.

## Prioritize companies where:

* manufacturing is core business;
* digital transformation is already funded;
* operational data has economic value;
* quality and efficiency matter;
* industrial systems are strategic.

---

## Avoid over-prioritizing companies based only on:

* brand recognition;
* company size;
* number of openings.

A large company without strategic fit may have lower expected ROI than a smaller industrial organization with a direct need.

---

# Relationship With Candidate Positioning

The strongest market narrative is:
