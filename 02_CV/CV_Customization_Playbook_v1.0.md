# CV Customization Playbook v1.0

## Purpose

Define the method for adapting Julian Cabrera Marceglia's CV to specific international opportunities without changing factual information.

The objective is to maximize alignment between:

* Job requirements.
* Professional positioning.
* Relevant evidence.

---

# Core Principle

The CV Master is the source of truth.

Customization changes:

* emphasis;
* ordering;
* keywords;
* evidence selection.

Customization does NOT change:

* employment history;
* responsibilities;
* achievements;
* technical facts.

---

# Customization Workflow

## Step 1 — Analyze Job Description

Extract:

### Role Identity

Identify the actual problem being solved.

Examples:

* Manufacturing systems improvement.
* Digital factory implementation.
* Industrial data management.
* Production optimization.

---

### Required Capabilities

Classify requirements:

## Manufacturing Domain

Examples:

* Automotive.
* Production processes.
* Engineering changes.
* Continuous improvement.

## Digital Systems

Examples:

* MES.
* Data platforms.
* Industrial applications.
* Automation.

## Data

Examples:

* SQL.
* Analytics.
* Reporting.
* Data architecture.

## Engineering

Examples:

* Metrology.
* GD&T.
* SPC.
* Root cause analysis.

# Step 3 — Evidence Mapping

For each requirement:

Create:

Requirement → Evidence

Example:

Requirement:

"Experience with manufacturing data systems."

Evidence:

"Designed and implemented centralized Measurement Data Management Platform integrating CMM, FARO and manual inspection data."

---

# Step 4 — Bullet Optimization

Prioritize bullets according to:

1. Direct relevance.
2. Business impact.
3. Technical complexity.
4. Uniqueness.

---

# Step 5 — Keyword Alignment

Include relevant terminology from the job description when truthful.

Examples:

Original:

"Measurement Data Management Platform"

Possible adaptation:

"Industrial Data Management Platform for Manufacturing Systems"

Only when technically accurate.

---

# Customization Limits

Never:

* Add technologies not used.
* Claim ownership of systems not developed.
* Inflate role scope.
* Change job titles dishonestly.

---

# Application Preparation Checklist

Before submitting:

## CV

* Correct variant selected.
* First third matches role.
* Keywords aligned.
* Achievements visible.

## LinkedIn

* Positioning consistent.
* Featured projects relevant.

## Message

* Company-specific.
* Role-specific.
* Evidence-based.
