* Adopted by quality and manufacturing personnel in production operations.

# Real-Time Quality Prioritization System

**Manufacturing Intelligence | Predictive Analytics | Decision Support System**

Implemented a real-time decision-support system that transforms dimensional measurement information into prioritized operational actions through statistical process capability analysis and predictive quality risk assessment.

The system extends manufacturing data capabilities from information management into analytics-driven operational decision support.

## Problem

Manufacturing environments generate large volumes of dimensional measurements. Traditional statistical reports describe current process conditions but require manual analysis to determine where engineering attention should be focused and where future risks may emerge.

## Solution

Implemented an analytics pipeline capable of:

* Automatically processing dimensional measurement data.
* Calculating current process capability using Cp/Cpk analysis.
* Estimating future quality risk using process behavior trends.
* Ranking process characteristics according to operational priority.
* Providing real-time operational screeners.

Developed:

* Quality Analytics Engine.
* Current Process Capability Screener.
* Predictive Risk Screener.
* Hops to NG predictive indicator.

## Technical Architecture

Implemented using:

* DuckDB.
* Pandas.
* NumPy.
* SciPy.
* SQLite / CSV data layers.
* Power BI visualization.
* TOML configuration.

Designed with:

* Modular architecture.
* Non-invasive integration principles.
* Compatibility with existing manufacturing information environments.
* Autonomous monitoring capabilities.

## Impact

* Shifted quality monitoring from reactive analysis toward predictive prioritization.
* Enabled faster identification of critical process deviations.
* Supported early identification of future quality risks.
* Reduced manual effort required to analyze and prioritize large volumes of measurement data.
* Established a scalable framework for manufacturing intelligence capabilities.
