# Job Opportunity Assessment Framework v1.0

## Purpose

Define the evaluation process used before applying to international opportunities.

The objective is to maximize application quality by prioritizing roles with the highest probability of success.

---

# Core Principle

Not every available position is an opportunity.

A role should be pursued when:

* professional positioning is clear;
* evidence exists;
* compensation potential is relevant;
* career trajectory improves.

---

# Opportunity Evaluation Process

## Step 1 — Role Classification

Classify the opportunity into one primary family:

### Manufacturing Systems

Examples:

* Manufacturing Systems Engineer
* MES Engineer
* Factory Systems Engineer

---

### Digital Manufacturing

Examples:

* Digital Manufacturing Engineer
* Industry 4.0 Engineer
* Smart Factory Engineer

---

### Industrial Data

Examples:

* Manufacturing Data Engineer
* Industrial Analytics Engineer
* Manufacturing Intelligence Specialist

---

### Manufacturing Software Solutions

Examples:

* Application Engineer
* Solution Engineer
* Industrial Software Consultant

---

# Step 2 — Strategic Fit Score

Score each category from 1 to 5.

| Category                       | Score |
| ------------------------------ | ----: |
| Manufacturing domain alignment |    /5 |
| Digital systems alignment      |    /5 |
| Evidence available             |    /5 |
| Compensation potential         |    /5 |
| International career impact    |    /5 |

Maximum:

25 points.

---

# Decision Rules

## 20-25 points

Priority opportunity.

Action:

Apply with customization.

---

## 15-19 points

Selective opportunity.

Action:

Apply only if strategic value exists.

---

## Below 15 points

Low priority.

Action:

Do not invest significant effort.

---

# Step 3 — Evidence Mapping

Create:

Requirement → Evidence

Example:

Requirement:

"Experience with manufacturing data systems."

Evidence:

Designed and implemented centralized Measurement Data Management Platform integrating CMM, FARO and inspection data.

---

# Step 4 — CV Selection

## Manufacturing Systems

Use:

Manufacturing Systems Engineer CV

* measurement data platform;
* manufacturing processes.

---

## Digital Manufacturing

Use:

Digital Manufacturing Engineer CV

Highlight:

* automation;

---

## Industrial Data

Use:

Industrial Data Manufacturing Intelligence CV

Highlight:

* data architecture;
* reporting;
* engineering analytics.

---

# Step 5 — Application Decision

Before applying answer:

## Why this company?

Specific reason required.

---

## Why this role?

Specific connection required.

---

## Why me?

Must include:

* manufacturing experience;
* digital capability;
* measurable achievement.

---

# Step 6 — Record Outcome

After applying record:

* date;
* role;
* company;
* CV version;
* response;
* learning.

---
