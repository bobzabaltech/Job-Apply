# Recruiter Outreach Framework v1.0

## Purpose

Define the communication strategy for initiating international career conversations.

The objective is not to ask for a job.

The objective is to create professional interest by presenting Julian Cabrera Marceglia as a Manufacturing Systems professional combining industrial experience and digital capabilities.

---

# Communication Principle

Avoid:

"I am looking for a new opportunity."

Prefer:

"I am exploring opportunities where my experience connecting manufacturing operations, industrial data and digital systems can create value."

---

# Core Positioning Message

## Short Version

I am a manufacturing professional with 21+ years of automotive industry experience, evolving from production and quality systems into industrial data management and manufacturing digitalization.

My focus is connecting real factory challenges with scalable digital solutions.

---

# Recruiter Initial Message

## LinkedIn Connection Request

Hi [Name],

---

# Application Message

When applying:

Do not repeat the CV.

Add context.

Structure:

1. Why this company.
2. Why this role.
3. Relevant evidence.

Example:

"My experience implementing a centralized measurement data management platform in an automotive manufacturing environment aligns strongly with your focus on digital manufacturing systems."

---

# Follow-Up Sequence

## Day 0

Initial contact.

## Day 7

Short follow-up with relevant context.

## Day 21

Final follow-up.

Avoid repeated generic messages.

---

# Personalization Rules

Every message should contain:

* Company reference.
* Role reference.
* Relevant experience.
* Specific reason for contact.

Avoid:

* Mass messaging.
* Generic job requests.
* Copy/paste without context.

---

# Success Metrics

Track:

* Connection acceptance rate.
* Reply rate.
* Recruiter conversations.
* Interviews generated.

---

# Optimization

If acceptance is high but replies are low:

Improve message relevance.

If acceptance is low:

Improve profile positioning.

If replies are positive but no interviews:

Review role targeting.
