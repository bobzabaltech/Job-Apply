# Application Tracking System v1.0

## Purpose

This document defines the operating system for managing international career opportunities.

The objective is to transform job searching into a measurable optimization process.

The system tracks:

* Target companies.
* Opportunities.
* Applications.
* Recruiter interactions.
* Interview progression.
* Market feedback.
* Strategy adjustments.

---

# Operating Principle

Applications are treated as market experiments.

Every opportunity provides information about:

* Market demand.
* Positioning effectiveness.
* Resume alignment.
* Skill perception.
* Compensation expectations.

A rejection is not only a negative outcome.

It is market feedback.

# Opportunity Record

Each opportunity must contain:

## Company Data

* Company name.
* Country.
* Industry segment.
* Business unit.
* Company size.
* Strategic relevance.

---

## Role Data

* Job title.
* Job URL.
* Role category.
* Required technologies.
* Required experience.
* Salary information.
* Work mode.

---

## Strategic Fit

Before applying answer:

Why does this role make sense?

Required fields:

* Target role.
* CV variant selected.
* Main positioning angle.
* Relevant case study.
* Evidence used.

# Application Quality Score

Before applying, score from 1-5:

| Criterion             | Score |
| --------------------- | ----: |
| Role alignment        |    /5 |
| Company strategic fit |    /5 |
| Salary potential      |    /5 |
| Evidence match        |    /5 |
| Career acceleration   |    /5 |

Minimum recommended score:

20/25

Applications below this score require justification.

# Pipeline Metrics

Track:

## Top Funnel

Number of:

* Companies researched.
* Roles identified.
* Applications submitted.

---

## Conversion Funnel

Measure:

Applications → Responses

Responses → Recruiter Calls

Recruiter Calls → Interviews

Interviews → Offers

---

# Interpretation Rules

## Low Response Rate

Possible causes:

* Wrong target companies.
* Weak keywords.
* Poor role alignment.
* LinkedIn discoverability issues.

Do not immediately conclude lack of skills.

---

## Interviews Without Offers

Possible causes:

* Positioning narrative.
* Salary expectations.
* Technical communication.
* Interview preparation.

---

## Strong Response Rate

Increase:

* Similar roles.
* Similar companies.
* Same CV variant.

---

# Networking Strategy

Applications should be complemented with:

* Recruiter connections.
* Hiring manager outreach.
* Industry discussions.
* Technical content.

Priority:

Quality over quantity.

---

# Weekly Review

Every week analyze:

## Market Signals

* Number of applications.
* Response rate.
* Interview rate.
* Common requirements.
* Salary signals.

---

## Strategy Adjustments

Possible changes:

* CV wording.
* Target industries.
* Geographic priority.
* Role selection.
* LinkedIn positioning.

---

# Success Metric

The objective is not:

Maximum applications.

The objective is:

Maximum probability of obtaining a high-value international offer.
